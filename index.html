Trigger is the smaller of 20% of BSLs or 200 BSLs from a single project, CAIs included. Source: Scope Change & Budget Modification Guidance, p.4; FAQ V22, 9.7, p.52

How this tool routes, with sources:

  • Reason Codes 1 to 3 are mandatory during the period of performance and carry direct authority, reported via the SAR. Scope Change & Budget Modification Guidance, p.4; FAQ V22, 2.40, p.41
  • Reason Codes 4 to 6 carry direct authority only if the drop stays at or below the smaller of 20% / 200, CAIs included; above that a New No BEAD Locations request is required. Scope Change & Budget Modification Guidance, p.4; FAQ V22, 9.7, p.52
  • Reason Code 7 requires NTIA approval before moving the BSLs to the No BEAD list, regardless of count, and presupposes NTIA has already denied an extraordinary circumstances request. FAQ V22, 9.7 n.8, p.52; Scope Change & Budget Modification Guidance, p.4
  • Any change in technology type, even partial, requires NTIA approval regardless of count. Scope Change & Budget Modification Primer, Table 1
  • The baseline rolls: each approved reduction becomes the new approved project area, so drops are not cumulative against the original Final Proposal. FAQ V22, 9.7, p.52
  • A scope change request runs through NGP and requires resubmission of all Final Proposal CSVs, budget documentation, and narrative responses; wait for the NOA amendment or administrative letter before implementing. Scope Change & Budget Modification Guidance, pp.6, 17; Primer, Change Request Submission Process
One open seam to confirm with your FPO: FAQ V22, 2.40 (p.41) states Reason Code 6 may be used throughout the period of performance for locations that cannot or should not be built, while FAQ 9.7 still groups RC 6 with the threshold-gated RC 4 to 7 set. If a drop leans heavily on RC 6, confirm with your FPO whether those locations count toward the 20% / 200 calculation before relying on the self-service path.

Always confirm with your FPO and the current Scope Change & Budget Modification Guidance. This tool is a routing aid, not a substitute for the guidance. Primer: "Eligible Entities must not rely on the information in this primer alone."
